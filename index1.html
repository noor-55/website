Fascinating Facts About Sea Turtles

Ancient Survivors:
Sea turtles have been swimming the oceans for more than 100 million years, making them older than many species of dinosaurs. These ancient mariners have witnessed the evolution of life on Earth and have adapted to survive across millennia.
